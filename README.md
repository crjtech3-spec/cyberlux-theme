# Cyber Lux — Merchant User Guide

Welcome to **Cyber Lux** — a premium Shopify theme by CRJ Studios designed for elegant product storytelling and high-converting product merchandising.

This repository contains the user-facing theme documentation your store team needs to configure and manage the theme.

Important links
- Full user manual: `docs/USER_GUIDE.md`
- Sections quick reference: `docs/SECTIONS.md`
- Theme settings overview: `docs/SETTINGS.md`
- Demo presets & examples: `docs/PRESETS.md`
- Support: `https://github.com/crjtech3-spec/cyberlux-theme/issues` or `cyberluxbycrj@gmail.com`

Quick tasks for store admins
1. Upload your logo: `Theme settings > Favicon & Logo > Logo`
2. Choose a color palette: `Theme settings > Colors > Theme color palette`
3. Add or reorder homepage sections: `Customize theme > Sections`
4. Preview a preset: open `listings/<preset>/index.*.json` in Theme Editor
5. Replace demo products with your catalog in the demo store

Who this guide is for
- Store owners and merchandisers who will manage content and visual identity.
- Content editors who will add homepage sections and update product content.

What you will *not* find here
- Developer-only instructions (CLI, Lighthouse, packaging). Those have been removed from the main user docs to avoid confusion.
